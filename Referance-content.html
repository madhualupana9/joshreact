WELCOME TO

JOSHITHA INFRATECH PRIVATE LIMITED

JOSHITHA Infratech Private Limited is a company established in 2010 as a Civil, Electrical, Mechanical, Design, Engineering and Construction company. The operations of the company commenced from FY2017 and presently involved in diverse construction activities. It is proud to state that it has bagged significant work orders from various clients in the year of commencement of operations itself to execute projects. The company registered as Class-I Civil Contractor with Telangana Government and having Electrical “A” Grade Licence. The company has its beginnings by the amalgamation of common interest of its promoter. i.e., to provide services to the industry through skilled and experienced Engineering Professionals. During the execution of various projects, we keep on developing our infrastructure and an efficient team, to execute any forthcoming works to be taken up by us. We have also accumulated enough resources to handle any magnitude of works. Wherever we work, we coordinate with local populous and local officials for the smooth execution of the contract. Apart from top management team, which consists of core engineering and management professionals, JOSHITHA has retained experienced, talented and skilled personnel for its operations and work sites. JOSHITHA is gearing up to meet the demands of the construction industry, to play its role more effectively in the face of stiff competition.

DIRECTOR PROFILE

HSE

Joshitha Infratech is committed to:

1. Conduct all its activities in such a manner so as to avoid injuries to employees, subcontractors and the community;

2. Promote occupational health of its employees and sub-contractors.

3. Sustainable development by continuously improving its environmental practices and performance;

4. Company with all statutory requirements concerning Health, Safety and Environment;

5. Create a culture of learning and practicing Health, Safety and Environment systems, procedures and practices among all its employees and sub-contractors;

6. Providing awareness training to the concerned person who are directly associated with the construction activities at our project sites to ensure good HSE performance;

QA & QC

The objective of our Quality Management is to:

1. Ensure that projects meet or exceed the quality standards established by our clients as laid down in the contract documents.

3. Provide direction to all project participants regarding our quality policies, standards and procedures.
